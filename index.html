Dr. Lin earned a B.S. from Shanghai Maritime University (2011), an M.S. from Stony Brook (2013), and an M.S. and Ph.D. from Washington State University (2015, 2020). Her work spans machine learning, deep learning, human dynamics, behavior modeling, home/building security, biomedical AI, and smart environments.

Warm welcome. We strive to cultivate a research and learning space that is curious, collaborative, and creative. I’m open to invited talks, research collaborations, and part-time, advising, consulting, or visiting opportunities. Please feel free to reach out—new ideas and insightful perspectives are always valued.
